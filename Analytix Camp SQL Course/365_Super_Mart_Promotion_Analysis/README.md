# 365 Super Mart - Promotion Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
Analysis of promotional campaign effectiveness for a leading FMCG retailer, evaluating impact on sales revenue, units sold, and profit margins across stores, products, and promotion types.

## Business Objectives
- Measure incremental revenue and sales units from promotions
- Identify top-performing stores and underperforming locations  
- Compare effectiveness of different promotion types (BOGOF, Cashback, Discounts)
- Analyze product category response to promotional campaigns

## Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** - Data analysis and querying
- **Power BI** - Data visualization and dashboards
- **Excel** - Data validation and exploration

## Key Findings
- Cashback promotions generated highest incremental revenue (122.6M)
- BOGOF drives highest unit sales uplift (157K units)
- Geographic disparities reveal optimization opportunities
- Discount-based promotions underperform compared to value-based offers
